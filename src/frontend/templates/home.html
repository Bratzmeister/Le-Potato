Le Potato is an Open-Source Software LLM Inference Software mixing elegance and minimalism with a painless and familiar user experience.
You can work with your documents (RAG) using your favorite open source LLMs and embedding models with a wallpaper of your choice.
It is easy to configure and customize plus on top of this beauty made by Crunchy Potato. It is left under BSD 3-Clause License.
